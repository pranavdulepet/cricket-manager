How To Play

Owner Brief

Every franchise starts on equal money. Winning comes from timing, not just aggressive bidding.

  • Use the live hammer clock to react before the lot closes.
  • Set a proxy cap when you know your walk-away price.
  • Track roster pressure so you do not leave gaps at keeper, spin, or death pace.
  • Read rival heat cards. Some teams are price inflators, others are pure value hunters.
  • Once squads are built, run a local season simulation with points, playoffs, and a final.
  • The app autosaves locally in your browser. Nothing leaves this device.
  • Keyboard shortcuts: B bid · P proxy cap · U undo bid · S skip lot · Esc clear proxy.
